DESIGN OF A FUEL INJECTOR


Fuel Injector consists of different parts such as :

Lock nut, Adjusting screw, Spring,

Injector, Spindle, Fuel inlet,

Leak off, Nozzle.





SOME MAJOR PARTS AND THEIR USES ARE :


Fuel Filters : To prevent dust and abrasive particles from entering the pump and injectors thereby minimizing the wear and tear of the components.


Fuel Feed Pump : To supply fuel from the main fuel tank to the injection system.


Injection Pump : To meter and pressurize the fuel for injection.


Governor : To ensure that the amount of fuel injected is in accordance with variation in load.


Injector : To take the fuel from the pump and distribute it in the combustion chamber by atomizing it into fine droplets.
